HCL TechBee program is India's first employment program that can get you a job in the IT field only after the 12th century. The training period will be 12 months and the job offer after the training will be entry-level job in IT services and associate job roles. During the training, you will get a stipend of Rs. 10,000 per month or Sastra University. And the package offer will be between Rs. 1.7 lakhs per annum and Rs. 2.2 lakhs.
